Stats Legend

Performance Columns

Fish
Landed fish counted in the current stats scope.
Hours
Logged fishing time, lure time, flasher time, or setup time when that data exists.
Missing time data
Fish or trips exist, but no usable category hours were logged. Fish/hr, Time %, Efficiency, and Over are intentionally hidden.
Fish / hr
Fish divided by hours. This is the main efficiency metric when time is available.
Fish / trip
Fish divided by trips where that item or category appeared.
Time %
Percent of selected fishing time spent using that item or category.
Fish %
Percent of selected landed fish produced by that item or category.
Quiet while others hit
Trips where the lure was used, caught nothing, and another lure caught fish.
Only producer trips
Trips where that lure caught fish and no other lure caught fish.

Efficiency Signals

Efficiency
Fish % divided by Time %. Above 1 means it produced more fish than its share of time.
Over
Fish % minus Time %. Positive means overperforming; negative means underperforming.
Skunk
Percent of trips in that category with zero landed fish.
Lost
Lost fish count shown only when you turn on Include lost fish or a row has meaningful lost-fish data.

Confidence

Low
Fewer than 3 trips, or fewer than 5 hours.
Medium
3 to 5 trips and 5 to 15 hours.
High
6+ trips and 15+ hours.
Min sample
The Min trips and Min hours controls hide rows below your chosen sample size.

Labels

High efficiency
Strong return for the time spent.
Overused / low return
Gets a lot of time but produces a smaller share of fish.
Needs more data
Promising or weak result, but the sample is still small.
Consistent producer
Good fish count with a rate near or above average.
Watch list
Enough to track, but not clearly high efficiency, low return, or consistent yet.
Best observed rate
The highest fish/hour seen so far, even if confidence is still low.
Best reliable performer
The best fish/hour among rows with Medium or High confidence.
